Boiled Egg Recipe

Ingrideints:

  • Egg
  • water
  • Salt

Method:

  1. Boil water:
    • Add water to a pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add Egg:
    • Gently place the egg into the boiling water.
  3. Cook Egg:
    • Boil the egg for 8-10 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Serve:
    • Remove the egg, peel it, and serve hot.
    • Optionally, sprinkle a pinch of salt on top
